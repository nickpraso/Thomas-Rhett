Thomas Rhett Akins Jr. was born in Valdosta, Georgia, on March 30, 1990. Thomas Rhett is a country/rock musician. Son of musician Rhett Akins. went to college for communications but dropped out at the age of 20 to pursue music. Started to become big in 2012 and has continued that career till today. Married to Lauren Akins with two children of his own. One of his most popular songs "Mary Me" was about his fear of not telling Lauren how he felt about her, and watching her marry someone else. Thomas and Lauren where told they could not have kids, so they had set up an adoption, and after the adoption went through Lauren found out she was pregnant. one who they had adopted. Christian. His song Life changes describes the biggest moments of his life.
